Que-9 How is Cell Padding different from Cell Spacing? With Example.

Cell Padding is making space inside the cell and Cell Spacing is make space between the cell. For Example:-

Without Cellpadding

Name Country Salary
Vruti India 30000

With cellpadding

Name Country Salary
Vruti India 30000

Without cellspacing

Name Country Salary
Vruti India 30000

With cellspacing

Name Country Salary
